Page Title

Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Half an onion
  • a tin of sweetcorn
  • a few mushrooms
  • 1-2 eggs

Method

  1. Start cooking the rice however you want, and put oil in the frying pan
  2. Chop the onion into bits, any size really, doesn't matter particularly and throw into the frying pan.
  3. Chop the mushrooms and add to the onions.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, drain it off and add it into the pan.
  5. Tip the drained sweetcorn into the pan as well and stir it all up.
  6. Break the eggs into the pan(or beat them together in another bowl and tip into the frying pan) and stir the mixture up.
  7. At this point, you need to keep stirring the mixture, keeping the eggs moving. Keep going until the eggs have cooked, should take about 5-8 minutes
